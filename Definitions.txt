Neutral Zone regrouping: after leaving offensive zone, puck stays in neutral zone until successful offensive zone entry or opposition takes puck into defensive zone

Full Zone regrouping: after leaving offensive zone, our team chooses to enter the defensive zone before the first opposition takeover

E(shots| neutral zone) = shots on the opposition's net after a successful neutral zone regrouping
E(offensive time | neutral zone) = time in the offensive zone after a successful neutral zone regrouping
E(breakout time | neutral zone) = time in neutral or defensive zone until an offensive zone entry success
